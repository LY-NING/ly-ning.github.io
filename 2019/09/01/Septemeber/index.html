#宣讲会
Characteristics of a successful applicant

  • Ethical and high character
  • Proven English ability
  • Strong academic record
  • Academically and professionally focused
  • Demonstrated interest in area of study
  • Attention to detail and deadlines
  • An all-around good fit

Tip#1:Engage!Lead!Innovate!
How?

  • Take challenging academic curses
  • Engage in research
  • Get an internship or a job
  • Become a leader on campus
  • Network with professionals
  • Participate in competitions and projects

Tip#2:Work on your english proficiency
How?

  • DO NOT BE SHY
  • Talk to the international students
  • Watch American TV shows (Big Bang Theory, Gossip Girls, CSI, Friends)
  • Watch the news : BBC CNN Bloomberg CCTV
  • Read English Publications
  • Participate in foreign exchange programs

Tip#3:Develop your technical skills
WHAT? HOW？

  • Learn programming: C/C++/C#, Java, Python, R, Scala, Ruby, SQL, VBA
  • Refine your research and laboratory skills
  • Sharpen your statistics and data skills

Tip#4:Develop your professionalism
How?

  • Firm handshake
  • Dress and behave professionally and ethically
  • Showcase your professionalism online: Linkin

#Sample professional development sessions

  • Art of the Interview
  • Advanced Ethics
  • Dress for Success & Science of style
  • Successful storytelling
  • Time management
  • Stress Management
  • Making small talk
